Hart voor Internetvrijheid

English

Hart voor Internetvrijheid (Heart for Internetfreedom) is a not-for-profit foundation registered in the Netherlands. Our mission is freedom on the internet. We do this by stimulating the use of technology that has a possitive impact on the online freedom of speech and anonimity and which allows for circumvention of censorship.

Contact

For general enqueries, send an e-mail to the foundation's board of members at bestuur@hartvoorinternetvrijheid.nl.

If you had some issue with the traffic coming from one of our Tor-nodes, please get in touch with abuse@hartvoorinternetvrijheid.nl.
