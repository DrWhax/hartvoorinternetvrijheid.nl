Hart voor Internetvrijheid

English:

Hart voor Internetvrijheid (Heart for Internetfreedom), is a foundation registered in the Netherlands which mission it is to fight for a anti-censorship, pro anonymity and free Internet. We achieve this by running for example, Tor Exit Nodes. Which sometimes, generate awful traffic, we can't do much about this.
